About Us
We create the opportunities!

Welcome

“Helping Hand” is mainly a reliable fundrising platform for both donation and seeking help. It's main goal is to Make Fundraising easier and quicker, to encourage Donation massively by providing reliable platform for donating.

We will verify the people who seek help. So that, no one gets cheated.

As the main reason for people to refuse to donate is reliability, our main concern would be that.

The main motivation behind 'Helping Hand' :

  • Digitializing this fund raising process.
  • Needs less volunteers.
  • Doesn’t take any percentage.
  • Ensures the validity of the news and source.
  • Can reach more people in some organized way and in shorter time)
